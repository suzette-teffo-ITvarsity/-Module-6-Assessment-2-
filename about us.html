ABOUT US

Who is Auto4Sale

Auto 4 Sale is the recognised retail brand within Africa’s leading automotive group. Auto 4 Sale is a non-manufacturing business in the automotive sector with an unrivaled scale and scope in South Africa, coupled with a selected international presence primarily in the United Kingdom and Australia, as well as a limited presence in South East Asia, Southern and East Africa. Auto 4 Sale traces its roots back to 2010 when its founding company Imperial Holdings started as a humble motor dealership in downtown Johannesburg. Auto 4 Sale is renowned for its long-standing importer and retail partnerships with leading automotive manufacturers. Our nationwide footprint of strategically located dealerships in growing urban areas underpins our leading market share. We represent 15 OEMs through 145 vehicle dealerships, of which 21 commercial vehicle dealerships

Value and Vision

Value

Auto 4 Sale values is to be fair, accountable, driven and ensure that we operate in an environmentally friendly and responsible manner. This includes complying with rules and regulations, dealing at the highest levels of integrity and ethics, and ensuring business practices are non-discriminatory.

Vision

Auto 4 Sale vision is to improve people’s lives by envisioning, innovating and creating new avenues of access to leading edge mobility solutions (products and services) at competitive prices through our strong relationships with suppliers and principals, while ensuring sustainable value creation for all stakeholders. We aim to exceed employees’ and client expectations and become OEM’s trusted partner of choice in the geographies in which we operate.
